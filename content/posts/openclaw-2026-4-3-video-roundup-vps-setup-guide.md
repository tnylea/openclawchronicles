If you've been waiting for a comprehensive, up-to-date video walkthrough of OpenClaw, this week delivered. A new full-length tutorial published on April 2, 2026 — "OpenClaw Tutorial for Beginners – Full Setup Guide" — covers the complete deployment process from scratch, making it one of the most thorough beginner resources published in 2026.

## What the Tutorial Covers

The video at [youtube.com/watch?v=WDHgibiZ9S8](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WDHgibiZ9S8) takes viewers through every major phase of a production-ready OpenClaw deployment:

- **VPS provisioning** — Choosing and setting up a Linux VPS for self-hosted operation
- **API key configuration** — Connecting your preferred LLM provider (OpenAI, Anthropic, local models)
- **Telegram channel setup** — Pairing your agent with a Telegram bot for mobile access
- **Skill installation** — Installing and activating AgentSkills to extend your agent's capabilities
- **Security hardening** — Locking down SSH, configuring exec approval policies, and protecting your gateway

## Why This Matters Now

OpenClaw's architecture has evolved considerably since early 2026. Recent releases — including v2026.3.31 through v2026.4.2 — changed how plugins load, how exec approvals are managed, and how node pairing works. Many older setup guides are now partially outdated, making this April 2026 tutorial particularly valuable for new users who want accurate, current instructions.

The timing is good. OpenClaw's install base has grown rapidly, and the community frequently asks for a single "start here" resource. This video fills that gap.
