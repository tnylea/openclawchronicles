## Why So Many VPS Tutorials Right Now?

The surge in VPS-focused content reflects a real shift in how people are using OpenClaw. Running it on a laptop means it's only available when your machine is on and awake. Running it on a VPS — or any dedicated machine — unlocks the always-on autonomous agent behavior OpenClaw was actually designed around: proactive check-ins, scheduled automations, inbox monitoring, calendar nudges.

NVIDIA's NemoClaw announcement earlier this month (targeting GeForce RTX PCs and DGX systems as dedicated agent hardware) signals that the industry sees "dedicated compute for your agent" as the natural next step. The VPS crowd is already living that future, just more affordably.
